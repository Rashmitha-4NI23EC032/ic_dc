About IC Design Club

IC Design Club (ICDC) is a student-driven technical club of the Department of ECE, NIE Mysuru, focusing on VLSI, semiconductor technology, and integrated circuit design through hands-on learning.

Vision

To build strong foundations in IC design and semiconductor technology.

Mission

Empower students with practical skills, innovation, and industry exposure.

Activities

Workshops, project expos, hackathons, FDPs, and expert talks.
