About NIE – IUCEE

NIE–IUCEE is an initiative to improve engineering education through faculty development, student skill enhancement, and global collaboration.

Vision

To transform engineering education by adopting global best practices.

Mission

  • Enhance teaching–learning processes
  • Encourage innovation and research
  • Strengthen industry–academia interaction

Activities

  • Faculty Development Programs (FDPs)
  • Workshops and training programs
  • Student skill development initiatives
